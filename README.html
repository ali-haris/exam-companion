Why Exam Companion?

  • Overwhelming syllabus before exams
  • Poor time management & procrastination
  • Uncertainty about progress leads to stress
  • Balancing study with life is hard

Without a clear plan, students waste time, lose focus, and feel anxious.

Our Solution

  • Parses course material from PDFs or pasted text
  • Calculates available time by exam date & busy hours
  • Generates a personalized daily schedule with AI
  • Export to PDF or Google Calendar for tracking

Result: a realistic, stress-free plan tailored to you.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: Streamlit
  • Backend: Python, OpenAI API
  • Deployment / Hosting: Hugging Face Spaces
  • Libraries & Tools: Claude AI, PyMuPDF, Pandas, DateTime, etc.

How to Use

  1. Enter exam date, available hours, and busy hours (sleep, commute, etc.).
  2. Upload your course content (PDF) or paste text.
  3. Click Generate to create your AI-powered study plan.
  4. Download the plan as PDF or export to Google Calendar.

Limitations

  • Rate limits on large PDFs
  • Limited customization for daily available hours

Future Work

  • Multi-language support for global accessibility
  • Better AI parsing for complex PDFs
  • Partial study plans (e.g., topic difficulty weighting)
  • Direct Google Calendar API syncing
  • Mobile-friendly responsive layout
